GIS Formula Boundary Files
HUD has developed formula grantee boundary files, designed for use in a Geographic Information System (GIS) mapping system. These are not map images.
While the Census Bureau provides GIS boundary files for many geographic components, the CPD formula grantee boundaries require special geography for which the boundary files are not available through the Census Bureau.
